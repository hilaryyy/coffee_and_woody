coffee and woody

welcome to coffee and woody's world!

two shiba inus. endless adventures. a home filled with eggs, naps, and unconditional love

coffee

coffee is a female shiba inu born on november 10, 2020 in osaka, japan.

her zest for life is matched only by her love for fruits and eggs.

she finds pure joy in life's simplest pleasures -- eating and sleeping.

once strinkingly slim, she now embraces her plush, cozy lifestyle with effortless grace.

coffee rarely stirs from her slumber, rising only for meals or to relocate to another favorite nap spot.

woody

woody is an energetic shiba inu born in tokyo, japan on april 4, 2021 -- right on children's day!

the moment someone walks through the door or rustles a snack bag, he lights up with excitement.

woody loves following his hoomans around the house, always ready for a treat or a bit of attention.

sometimes, his curiosity gets the better of him, especially around new people, but it's all part of his charm!
